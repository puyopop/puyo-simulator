Puyo Puyo Simulator

A simple Puyo Puyo game simulator built with Deno and TypeScript

Controls:

  • Left/Right Arrow: Move horizontally
  • Down Arrow: Move down faster
  • Up Arrow: Rotate clockwise
  • Z Key: Rotate counter-clockwise
  • Space: Hard drop
  • U Key: Undo move
  • R Key: Redo move
